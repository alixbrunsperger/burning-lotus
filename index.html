The Sao-Lim Hood Karpai is a traditional style from southern China and was taught to monks when they served as mercenaries or escorts. This is why this style is defined by low positions and an optics oriented towards purified efficiency
 
A class always starts with a warm-up. Then comes the basic training, then the taos and the applications. We end the course with a short meditation
